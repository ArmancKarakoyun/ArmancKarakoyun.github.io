ABOUT ME

Hello there! I was born in 1994. I finished elementary in Van Atatürk Elementary School and high school in İzmir Şehit Ali Karaoglan High School. Then, I moved to İstanbul for university education in 2012. I have graduated from Mechanical Engineering & Energy Systems Engineering (double major program) at Istanbul Bilgi University. In 2018, I was granted the Award of Best Senior Project in Mechanical Engineering. I`m currently a master student in the field of Robotics Engineering at the University of Science and Technology, as well as I am engaged in several project-based research work within KIST since August 2020. I am working as a in Dr. Kim Chunwoo`s research team. My duty in the group is developing surgical tools for robot-assisted bi-portal endoscopic surgery.
